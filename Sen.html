Sentinels clean sweep PRX in the AfreecaTV Grand Finals

December 12, 2023

Seoul, South Korea-The Sentinels emerged triumphant in a thrilling showdown at the AfreecaTV Valorant League Tournament, securing a decisive 3-0 victory in the grand finals against VCT Champions runners-up, Paper Rex.

The grand finals showcased Sentinels' exceptional gameplay and strategic prowess as they dominated each map. The team's stellar performance left spectators and fans in awe, solidifying their reputation as one of the top Valorant teams in the world.

North American organization Sentinels has continued its ten-game off-season unbeaten streak following a Championship win at the AfreecaTV VALORANT league tournament, which saw Sentinels take down the runners-up of Champions 2023, Paper Rex, 3-0 in the grand final. 

Sentinels have been the most active team from North America during the OFF//SEASON, competing in three show matches, one regional tournament, and one international event. At the AfreecaTV tournament, which concluded on December 10, Sentinels beat Paper Rex, twice, DRX, and Team Liquid to win the trophy. 

Sentinels accomplished this against the stacked field that included some of the most accomplished organizations in the world throughout 2023. Sentinels took down not just one, but two Champions at the tournament. Team Liquid and Paper Rex were the victors of the respective regional leagues in EMEA and the Pacific region. Korean-based team DRX is also known for its long-term dominance in the country. The team also finished second in the VCT Pacific regional and third in the VCT LOCK//IN event in Brazil. 

The victory over Paper Rex in the grand final marked the tenth win in a row for the Sentinels VALORANT team, which during the off-season brought in Mouhamed “johnqt” Ouarid as the in-game leader and Jordan “Zellsis” Montemurro as the super “sixth man.” Sentinels have yet to drop a match with Zellsis in the starting roster.
